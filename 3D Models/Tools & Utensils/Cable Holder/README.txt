# Summary

Organize your cables and prevent them from constantly falling off your desk with this cable organizer. Designed to be low profile yet elegant and prints without any supports. 

Stick it to your desk with double sided tape. Print as many as you want and stick them side by side to have all your wires neatly organized. 

The slower your printing speed the better the finish and strength. 
Recommended: 50-60mm/s
